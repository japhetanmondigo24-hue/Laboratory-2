Student 2

Focus: Design & Creative Development

Day 1: Personal Insight

Explored new design inspirations and practiced UI layouts. Today's focus was on understanding color theory and how it impacts user experience. I discovered some amazing design patterns on Dribbble and incorporated several techniques into my projects. Design is truly the art of problem-solving!

Day 2: Prototype Development

Started working on interactive prototypes using Figma. Creating user flows and wireframes helped me visualize the entire user journey. I realized how important it is to test designs with actual users before finalizing. Feedback is gold for improving design quality.

Day 3: Responsive Design

Tackled the challenge of making designs responsive across different screen sizes. Mobile-first design approach changed my perspective entirely. I learned that constraints can be creative opportunities—working within mobile limitations pushed me to simplify and prioritize the most important features.

Day 4: Brand Development

Today I worked on creating a cohesive brand identity for a project. From logo design to color palettes to typography—every element matters. I'm learning that successful design isn't just about making something pretty; it's about creating a complete experience that communicates the brand's values clearly.
